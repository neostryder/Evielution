This simulated placeholder marks the game board as a sketch-heavy room rather than a finished catalog. It is meant to imply a table full of half-tested mechanics, world hooks, and "wait, what if" systems ideas.

## What belongs here later

Real entries might include short pitch blurbs, component mockups, playtest notes, or downloadable one-page prototypes. The tone should stay playful and invitational, with enough structure to show the design brain at work.

## Why keep it visible early

Games are one of the best bridges between writing, music, and worldbuilding on the site. Even a placeholder note helps signal that Evielution is not only a portfolio of polished artifacts - it is also a workshop for systems and play.
