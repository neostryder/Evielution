This simulated placeholder holds space for the media side of Evielution: recordings in progress, short video experiments, spoken reflections, and anything that wants to live as sound or moving light instead of text on a page.

## What could land here

Later, this room might host embedded audio, rehearsal clips, annotated demos, tiny film experiments, or podcast-sized thoughts captured before they evaporate.

## Design intent

The media room should feel adjacent to the music room, but broader in energy - less sheet music, more signal and atmosphere.
