The workshop is a foundational part of the site's personality, and this simulated placeholder exists to make that visible early. It gives permission for unfinished work to appear with dignity instead of being hidden until it behaves.

## What the workshop stands for

This room is where draft fragments, visual experiments, half-built systems, and emotional sketches can stay accessible without pretending to be complete. It is the opposite of a portfolio's "only the highlights" instinct.

## Later possibilities

Real workshop entries might become changelogs, patch notes, draft diaries, process galleries, or little "currently testing" snapshots that evolve over time.
